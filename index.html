Explore the intense and thrilling competition that tests the limits of its contestants. Survivor is a popular show on CBS that has been running since 2015. It's nearing it's 50th season and shows no signs of slowing down.

Host Jefft Probst takes contestants and divides them into tribes to test their ability to outwit, outplay, and outlast. Contestants compete in competitions designed to push the physical and mental limits.
